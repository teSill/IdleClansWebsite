Game Features

True Idle MMORPG

Deep idle RPG with multi-player at it's core. Grab your friends and start leveling over 12 skills, complete daily quests and challenges, and take down combat bosses in a variety of biomes, trade between players, and race to the top of the high scores.

Player Guilds

Form your own guild or join an existing one to complete guild quests, build your own guild HQ, and join the battle to become the #1 Guild in Idle Clans.

Bossing & Skilling

With over 12 skills and 120 levels to grind there's alway something to be 'idling'! Once you've got your gear and stats ready, fight NPCs for keys to dungeons where you can face end-game bosses for unique loot.
